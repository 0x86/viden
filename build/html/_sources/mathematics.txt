Matrices: Transpose
*******************
Interchange rows and columns 

.. math::

   \mathbf{A} = 
    \begin{pmatrix}
    1 & -2 & 0\\
    0 & 3 & 5
    \end{pmatrix}


    \mathbf{A^T} = 
    \begin{pmatrix}
	1 & 0 \\
	-2 & 3 \\
	 0 & 5
    \end{pmatrix}
